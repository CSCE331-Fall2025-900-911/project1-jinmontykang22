The 12th Can

Currently, I have been a member of The 12th Can for 2 years. This is a student-led food pantry available to students, faculty, and staff. Their mission to eliminate hunger at A&M and raise awareness on food insecurity on campuses is a message I highly value.

This is an opportunity for me to contribute to the community and help support an organization that positively impacts others' lives. I get to work for a cause that I can wholeheartedly agree with. Most of my work is during pantry openings and performing other simple tasks. Even the mundane work helps keep things running, so I'm content with what I do.
